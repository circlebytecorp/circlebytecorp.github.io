Welcome to Your Ultimate Daily Vocabulary Companion!

DailyLingo is designed for English learners of all levels—travelers, expats, and international students—who want to build practical vocabulary across everyday topics in a fun and efficient way.

What’s Inside:

  • Daily-Life Categories: Each category reflects real-world situations you encounter every day.

  • Essential Words per Category: Carefully selected for relevance and usefulness. Each word includes:

    • A clear, concise definition
    • A practical, daily-use dialogue showing the word in context
    • Audio playback of the word, its definition, and the dialogue—for correct pronunciation

  • Multilingual Support: Every word, definition, and dialogue can be translated into multiple different languages, making it perfect for bilingual learners and global users. (Languages supported so far: Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Russian, and Spanish)

Who Should Use This App?

  • English Language Learners: Expand your vocabulary with real-life examples and accurate pronunciation.
  • Travelers: Learn the English words you’ll actually use on your journeys.
  • Expats & Immigrants: Gain practical language support for everyday life situations.
  • Students: Reinforce vocabulary with interactive examples and translations.

Whether you're brushing up on your language skills or starting from scratch, DailyLingo gives you the words that matter—when and where you need them most.

We continuously update daily words and dialogues to keep your learning fresh and relevant.
