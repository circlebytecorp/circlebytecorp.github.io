Welcome to your ultimate daily vocabulary companion!

The DailyLingo is designed for English learners of all levels, travelers, expats, and international students who want to build practical vocabulary across everyday topics in a fun, efficient way.

What's Inside:

  • Daily-Life Categories: Each category reflects real-world situations you encounter every day.
  • Essential Words per Category: Carefully selected to be relevant and useful, each word is accompanied by:
    • A clear and concise definition
    • A daily-use dialogue showing how the word is used in context
    • Audio playback of the word, its definition, dialogue, recorded for proper pronunciation
  • Multilingual Support: Every word, definition, and dialog can be translated into 5 different languages making it perfect for bilingual learners or global users. (Languages supported: [Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Russian and Spanish]).

Who Should Use This App?

  • English language learners: Expand your vocabulary with real-life examples and pronunciation.
  • Travelers: Learn the words you'll actually use in English.
  • Expats & Immigrants: Get practical English support for everyday life.
  • Students: Reinforce vocabulary with examples and translations.

Whether you're brushing up on your language skilld or starting from scratch, this app gives you the words that matter- when and where you need them most.
